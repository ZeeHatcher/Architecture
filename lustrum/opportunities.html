Opportunities

We are always on the lookout for innovative architects, engineers, planners and project managers, offering great career opportunities and the chance to work on a vast array of projects, both in Japan and overseas. With over 2,500 people at offices in Japan, China, Korea, Vietnam, Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates (Dubai), the Nikken Group is truly an experience in international collaboration. We also offer an exciting and diverse work environment, with staff from over 15 countries including Japan, China, South Korea, India, New Zealand, Russia, Germany, Iran, Syria, Taiwan, US, Canada, Brazil, Uruguay and Vietnam.

Scholarship

Nikken Sekkei Scholarship for Aspiring Architects

This scholarship is aimed at providing students that aspire to go into the architectural field.

Eligibility

  • Enrolled in architectural courses
  • Only short-listed candidates will be called for an interview
  • Open to bachelor degree or equivalent recognised qualification holders
  • Familiar with Japanese culture, and loves Japan.

Value of Scholarship

25% fee waiver on total program fee

Criteria

  • Bachelor degree graduate with CGPA of 3.33 and above
  • Recipients of the bursary must maintain a CGPA of 3.00 and above throughout the program of study to continue to be eligible for the bursary
  • Personal qualities and character and the extent to which the applicant has demonstrated exceptional leadership qualities
  • Participation and achievements in extra-curricular activities including sports and community services

General Submissions

Please submit your CV/resume, and portfolio (where relevant, no larger than A4 Maximum 3 pages) via e-mail. Clealy indicate the position and location of the position you are applying for. * If you are applying for jobs for Nikken Sekkei Tokyo Headquarters, please download our CV format and submit it with your portfolio via e-mail.

(Proficient in Japanese-Language is preferred for the current opening position)
