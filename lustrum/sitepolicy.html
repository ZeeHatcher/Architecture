Handling of personal information

Nikken Sekkei respects personal information and is aware of the need to store and manage it in an appropriate manner. Personal information is protected in line with the following policy;

  1. Personal information will be acquired in an appropriate manner.
  2. All personal information provided by a person will be handled within the scope of the purpose of use identified to the person.
  3. No personal information will be provided to third parties by The Company except in the following cases;

    • the consent of the person is obtained

    • the provision of information is based on laws

  4. The integrity of personal information is maintained and securely managed.
  5. The Company shall comply with all laws and regulations governing the handling of personal information. The Company maintains an internal personal information protection system which is reviewed and updated as required.

Handling of personal recruitment information

Information handling policy

For the purpose of appropriate protection of personal information received during the recruitment process, in addition to adhering to all laws and regulations governing the protection of information, Nikken Group maintains an internal personal information protection system which is continuously updated.

Definition of personal information

Personal information is defined as information directly relating to an individual that allows them to be identified such as name, birthdate, address, phone number and e-mail address etc. (includes any information on which an identification may not be made on the basis of that information alone but by matching it with other information an individual may be identified).

Collection of personal information and purpose of use:

  1. Nikken Group will only use personal information acquired for the following purposes. Information will only be collected directly from an individual or with the consent of the individual using legal and fair methods.
    1. For notification of or registration for seminars
    2. For notification of company information
    3. For recruitment notification
    4. For registering interest in employment
    5. For employment selection
    6. For confirming skills and experience to determine competence for a position
    7. For office processing such as contact details for employment selection
    8. For notification of ceremonies for prospective employees, employment health checks and events etc.
    9. For workforce management after employment
  2. When Nikken Group is utilizing personal information for the purposes of recruitment and the person concerned supplies notification that the application is withdrawn, the use of personal information will also cease from that point.

Management of personal information

Nikken Group will manage personal information under a fully-equipped internal management system operated by thoroughly trained staff to ensure that personal information collected is not lost, destroyed, falsified, leaked or accessed illegally.

Provision of personal information to a third party

Nikken Group will not provide personal information to third parties other than to recruitment outsourcers without prior consent of the individual concerned unless required to by law.

Outsource management

Nikken Group will ensure that any outsourcers employed for recruitment activities such as processing personal information, forwarding documents or emails to applicants or carrying out questionnaires are fully aware and compliant with The Company policies.
